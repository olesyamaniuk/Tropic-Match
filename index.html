How to Play

  • Solve Match 3 Puzzles – Match fruits and items to clear levels and collect

  • Earn Stars – Use stars to unlock and renovate various parts of your resort.

  • Decorate with Style – Choose from multiple options to make your island feel like home.

  • Follow the Story – Help characters discover their past, connect with others, and find new beginnings.

  • Use Boosters – Power through difficult levels with hammers, bombs, and rainbow fruits.

  • Join Events – Participate in seasonal and weekly events to win unique decorations and rewards.

  • Unlock Islands – Discover new locations filled with different puzzles, decorations, and storylines.
